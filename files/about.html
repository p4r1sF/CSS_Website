Who We Are

The Computer Science Society (CSS) Club is dedicated to promoting the STEM field, specifically computer science, technology, and mathematics. We work to provide resources for any student, alumni, and professionals regardless of an individual's degree of knowledge or commitment in the field of computer science. CSS meets and hosts events that provide opportunities for networking, educational, and professional development related to computer science and technology. Our mission is to provide students with the skills, resources, motivation, and training necessary to become successful leaders in STEM.

We aim to help every individual grow as a person and as as tech professional to feel confident in their tech skills once they graduate. On top of that, we are a community of like-minded individuals who are eager to network and succeed. Come join us at the CSS Club at John Jay College.

What We Do During The Semester

Learn New Languages

Join us for exciting workshops where you’ll be able to learn new programming lanuages. You’ll also learn how to ace technical interviews and grow your confidence.

Find The Best Resources

Over the semesters, we have noticed many Computer Science students are pointing out that there is so much information out there related to finding internships, jobs, and coding resources. For that reason we have compiled plenty of resources ranging from CodePath’s amazing free courses to Codeacademy.

Conquer LeetCode

We know that in most sectors in the tech field, you will have to go on the “LeetCode Grind.” Hence, we have LeetCode challenge contests so you can build up the necessary Data Structure skills to complete more LeetCode problems and ace your technical interview. We also have a LeetCode bot in our discord to help you out.

Discussion With Your Peers

Join our club’s Discord server to collaborate, chat, have a good time with your peers, and stay up to date with anything club related. Be on the lookout for any cool club giveaways.
