Who We are

The Computer Science Society (CSS) is dedicated to promoting the STEM field, specifically computer science, technology, and mathematics. We work to provide resources for any student, alumni, and professionals regardless of an individual's degree of knowledge or commitment in the field of computer science. CSS meets and hosts events that provide opportunities for networking, educational, and professional development related to computer science and technology. Our mission is to provide students with the skills, resources, motivation, and training necessary to become successful leaders in STEM.

We aim at growing every individual as a person and a programmer that will feel confident in their coding skills once they graduate. On top of that, we are a community of like-minded individuals who are eager to succeed and network. Come and join us at the Computer Science Society Club at John Jay College.

What We Do During The Semester

Learn New Languages

Join us for exciting workshops where you’ll be able to learn new programming lanuages ranging from Python to Swift. On top of learning new languagues you’ll be able to also learn about acing and growing your confidence for Technical Interviews.

Fine The Best Resources

Over the semesters we have noticed many Computer Science students pointing out that their is so much information out their related to finding internships/coding resources, so we have compiled plenty of resources ranging from CodePath’s amazing free courses to Codeacademy.

Conquer LeetCode

We know that in most sectors in the Tech field, one way or another you will have to go on the “LeetCode Grind.” Hence, we have LeetCode Challenge Contests so you can build up those necessary Data Structure skills necessary to do more LeetCode problems and conquer your technical inteview. We also have a LeetCode bot in our discord to help out with your grind.

Discussion With Your Peers

Join our club’s Discord Server to collaborate, chat, and have a good time with your peers. Also, join to stay up to date on anything club related and any cool merchandise handouts.
